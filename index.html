ICHIGO KUROSAKI 黒崎一護

"I'm here to protect the Soul Society"

  • Anime :- BLEACH
  • Character name :- ICHIGO
  • Role :- PROTAGONIST 
    Ichigo Kurosaki is a high school student with the rare ability to see ghosts. 
    His life changes dramatically when he meets  Rukia Kuchiki and accidentally obtains the powers of a Soul Reaper. 
    From that point on, Ichigo takes on the responsibility of defending the living from Hollows (evil spirits) and guiding souls to the afterlife.

Ichigo Powers:
Shikai Zangetsu (斬月)
Ichigo’s Shikai is always in a released state and appears as a large cleaver-like blade. It allows him to perform Getsuga Tenshō, a crescent-shaped energy slash. Zangetsu reflects his raw, uncontrolled spiritual power early in the series.

Tensa Zangetsu (天鎖斬月)
His Bankai transforms into a slim black katana, boosting speed and precision. Unlike most Bankai, it condenses power rather than enlarging the weapon. In its true form, it embodies his full Shinigami, Hollow, and Quincy heritage.
